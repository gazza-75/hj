FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work out of hours? Yes I work whatever hours the job requires.
Can you work on a labour only basis? Of course I can as long as the materials are quality and suitable.
What happens to any waste at the end of the job? I would dispose of any & all waste and are fully SEPA registered to do so.
Can you work at heights? I have various ladders & scaffolding to allow me to work at height.
Is it just Joinery work you undertake? No, I can turn my hand to most things. So if you need some concreting, slabs or gravel, new kitchen sink, or integrated dish wash washer I am happy to install and plumb it in as well. I also offer a full ceramic tiling service.
